# Task 2: Phishing Email Analysis

## Objective
To identify phishing characteristics in a suspicious email.

## Sample Email
From: support@paypaI.com
Subject: Urgent: Your Account Has Been Limited!

Dear Customer,

We noticed suspicious activity on your account. Your account has been temporarily limited.

Please verify your account immediately by clicking the link below:
http://paypal-security-check.verify-user.ru

Failure to do so within 24 hours will result in permanent suspension.

Thank you,
PayPal Security Team

---

##  Analysis

### 1. Sender Address
- Email: support@paypaI.com
- Issue: Spoofed domain (PaypaI vs PayPal)

### 2. Suspicious Link
- URL: http://paypal-security-check.verify-user.ru
- Issue: Fake domain, not official PayPal

### 3. Urgency / Threat
- Message creates panic (24 hours warning)

### 4. Generic Greeting
- Uses "Dear Customer" instead of name

### 5. Grammar / Language
- Slight unnatural phrasing

### 6. Social Engineering
- Creates fear to force action

---

## Conclusion
This email is a phishing attempt designed to steal user credentials using spoofing, fake links, and urgency tactics.

---

## Prevention Tips
- Always verify sender email
- Never click unknown links
- Use official websites only
- Enable 2FA
