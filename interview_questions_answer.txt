# Cybersecurity Internship – Interview Questions & Answers

## 1. What is phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card details, or personal data by pretending to be a trusted entity.

---

## 2. How to identify a phishing email?

You can identify a phishing email by checking:

* Suspicious or fake sender email address
* Urgent or threatening language
* Unknown or mismatched links
* Spelling and grammar mistakes
* Generic greetings like “Dear Customer”

---

## 3. What is email spoofing?

Email spoofing is a technique where attackers fake the sender’s email address to make it appear as if the email is coming from a trusted source.

---

## 4. Why are phishing emails dangerous?

Phishing emails are dangerous because they can:

* Steal login credentials
* Access bank accounts
* Install malware
* Cause financial and data loss

---

## 5. How can you verify the sender’s authenticity?

* Check the email domain carefully
* Hover over links before clicking
* Contact the company through official website
* Analyze email headers

---

## 6. What tools can analyze email headers?

* MXToolbox
* Google Admin Toolbox
* Mailheader Analyzer

---

## 7. What actions should be taken on suspected phishing emails?

* Do not click any links
* Do not download attachments
* Report the email as phishing
* Delete the email immediately

---

## 8. How do attackers use social engineering in phishing?

Attackers use psychological tricks like:

* Creating urgency (e.g., “Your account will be blocked”)
* Fear (security alerts)
* Trust (pretending to be banks or companies)
  to manipulate users into taking quick action.
